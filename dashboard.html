What is Project Hub?

A platform where students can upload their hackathon-winning or ideathon-submitted projects. Every month, the top 3 projects will be featured and get a chance to receive mentorship, resources, or guidance.

How it works?

Upload: Share your hackathon or ideathon project.
Get Featured: Every month, top 3 projects are highlighted.
Get Support: Selected projects receive mentorship and help.
